# Canva Pin Template Prompts

Copy one prompt at a time into Canva. Replace the destination URL in the final design or Pin settings, not inside the visual.

## 1. Main quiz invitation Pin

**Format:** 1000 × 1500 px vertical Pinterest Pin, 2:3 ratio.

**Text:** Headline: “What’s Your Room Aesthetic?” Subheadline: “Take the free quiz and find your dream room style.” CTA: “Start the quiz”.

**Visual style:** Warm neutral colors, cream and muted rose accents, cozy modern bedroom feeling, subtle paper texture, editorial serif headline and clean sans serif supporting type.

**Layout:** Large headline in the top third, airy abstract bedroom or three-frame room moodboard in the center, small color swatches, rounded CTA at the bottom.

**Design constraints:** Strong mobile readability, short text only, no email field, no fake button shadows, no clutter, no logos, no watermarks, no childish clip art.

## 9. Dorm room decor Pin

**Format:** 1000 × 1500 px vertical Pinterest Pin.

**Text:** Headline: “Dorm Room Decor, But Make It You” Subheadline: “Find your style before move-in shopping.” CTA: “Take the free quiz”.

## 10. Small bedroom makeover Pin

**Format:** 1000 × 1500 px vertical Pinterest Pin.

**Text:** Headline: “Small Room? Start With Your Style.” Subheadline: “Get a personalized makeover direction in 12 quick questions.” CTA: “Take the free quiz”.

**Visual style:** Airy small bedroom with clear zones, warm lamp, useful storage, soft palette; simple plan-sheet accents and rounded labels.
